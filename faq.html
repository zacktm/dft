    Frequently asked questions

    General

    What exactly is a Crypto Currency?

    A digital currency in which encryption techniques are used to regulate the generation of units of currency and verify the transfer of funds, operating independently of a central bank.

    What Makes it Different from Normal Currencies?

    Digital currency can be used to buy things electronically. It’s much like conventional dollars, euros or yen. Although these currencies can also be traded digitally, they rely on a physical presence and centralization to control and monitor their existence.The most important of aspect of digital currency and the feature that makes it different to conventional money is that it is decentralized. No single institution or person controls any digital currency network.

    What is a Draftcoin?

    A Draftcoin is a cryptocurrency that is used exclusively on BTCDraft as the main credit system.

    Where can I purchase Draftcoins?

    Draftcoins can be purchased through the BTCDraft website once logged in, or they can be purchased through the Paypal for canadians and through The Draftcoin Exchange

    What Cryptocurrencies can you buy Draftcoins with?

    Bitcoin is the main Currency at the moment but you can vote on our "Coin Voting" page to get your favorite Cryptocurrency added

    Why choose a Crypto currency instead of using Fiat?

    By using a Crypto currency we believe we are making a change. but the following are some good points. Speed of Transactions, Cost of Transactions, Decentralization, Fraud Protection, Identity Theft Protection, Anonymity, No Need to Trust Anyone, and You Own Them!

    How do I deposit my Draftcoins?

    Generate a Draftcoin address with in your user profile, once created deposit coins from a sucessfully synced DFT wallet to the Exact address shown with-in the website.

    What's the minimum and maxium I can withdrawal?

    Those variables are based on the verification level for each individual user. Please refer to the fees section

    What fees do BTCDraft charge?

    BTCDraft Charges a 1% fee on all jackpots won, a variable withdrawal fee is added to increase network speed and transactions

    Is my personal information encryted and secured?

    All information is stored in secure Sha256 encryption

    How do I get invited to the Draftcoin telegram channel?

    Telegram is Free to join, signup a t.me/btcdraftcoins

    What are my withdrawal limits on BTCDraft?

    Withdrawal limits are based on Verification levels with in the site. Once verified you will be informed of your new limit restrictions.

    Why isn't my withdrawal working?

    Some times networks become laggy because of low fee transactions, btcdraft charges a small tx fee to help increase the speed of these transactions.

    Why am I unable to receive the confirmation email for withdrawals?

    Please make sure to check you SPAM folder or Junk mail.

    How can I invite my friends to play?

    Each user has there very own unique Referral code located within your Profile page. Hand these out to receive a % of comission.

    Coin Voting

    How do I cast a vote?

    You may cast votes for your favorite Coins by purchasing DFT (Draftcoins) and clicking the corrisponding Vote button.

    How much does it cost to vote?

    Each Vote will cost 1 DFT (approx 10 Cents)

    How many votes before a coin is added?

    Every 2 weeks BTCDraft will review the top coin on the voting list. There isn't a specific amount of votes required for a coin to be added.

    What are the specifications for a coin to be added?

    For a coin to be added it must meet certain criteria. for example the coin must have enough volume and liquidity and the coin must be on exchanges.

    Game FAQ's

    How Often are the Game jackpots reset

    The game jackpots are reset automatically every 12-24 hours depending on game type.

    How do you win a High Score game?

    The high score games are won when a player succeeds in setting the #1 Highscore position

    What is the game Jackpot made up of?

    Every time a player fails to beat the new High score the funds are sent to the jackpot.

    What happens to the Jackpot funds when the game is rest?

    The funds are added to a weekly jackpot. Users can purchase raffle tickets to win this.

    What is the house edge?

    The house edge varries but is only present in the gabling games, the arcade type games have no house edge.
